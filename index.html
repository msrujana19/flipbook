Chikmaglur


2-2-2024

Chikmagalur, nestled in the Western Ghats of Karnataka, is known for its beautiful coffee plantations and green hills. It's a peaceful place with lovely views, perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature, serene landscapes, and rich cultural heritage, making it an ideal retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Puducherry


20-4-2024

Puducherry is a city in India, known for its unique blend of French colonial architecture and Indian culture.
It offers beautiful beaches, serene promenades, and a laid-back atmosphere. Visitors often enjoy exploring its charming streets, visiting historic churches, and experiencing a mix of French and Tamil cuisine

Paradise Beach

21-4-2024

Paradise Beach, located near Pondicheri, is a stunning stretch of golden sand and clear blue waters. Accessible by a short boat ride, it offers a peaceful escape with a scenic view of the ocean. The beach is perfect for relaxing, swimming, and enjoying the natural beauty of the coastline. It's a tranquil spot where you can unwind and soak in the serene surroundings.

Auroville

21-4-2024

Auroville is a special town near Pondicherry where people from different countries live together in harmony. Founded in 1968, it aims to promote peace and unity through sustainable living and innovative practices. The community values collaboration and cultural exchange, making it a unique place for personal growth and global understanding. Its peaceful environment and diverse residents create a unique living experience.

Dharmasthala

11-5-2024

Dharmasthala is a renowned religious town in Karnataka, India. It is famous for the Shri Manjunatha Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and is a key pilgrimage site for Hindus. The town is known for its hospitality and the tradition of providing free meals to visitors. Dharmasthala is also recognized for its commitment to social welfare and its role in preserving cultural heritage.
